Event Rules

Overview

The Cricket Player Management System is designed to manage players' selection across franchises in a sporting event, such as the IPL. The system allows event managers to manage player data, track franchise selections, and enforce event-specific rules.

Business Rules

  • User Types: Event Managers and Franchises.
  • Player Data: Includes role, country, strength ratings (batting, bowling, fielding), and initial price.
  • Franchise Functionality: Login, view player pool, and select players.
  • Event Manager Functionality: Player pool creation, rules, and franchise selection approval.
  • Rule Validation: Ensures compliance with budget limits and foreign player caps.

Tables

  • Franchise Information
  • Player Details
  • Administrator Information
  • Rules
  • Registration Details
  • Team Selection
  • Player Availability
  • Login Audit
  • Player Injury Details
  • Franchise Budget Allocation History
  • Player Auction History
  • Franchise Player Distribution by Role
  • Team Composition Summary

Outputs

  • Admin View: Franchise-wise and player-wise selections.
  • Franchise View: Available players, selected players, and rule compliance.
  • Player View: Strength-wise, country-wise, and availability status.
